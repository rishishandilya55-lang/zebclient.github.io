About

What Zeb Client
is all about.

01

Built for Players

Every feature in Zeb Client was designed with real players in mind. No corporate roadmaps, no investor deadlines — just a tool built to make your Minecraft experience genuinely better. If a feature doesn't work, it doesn't ship.

02

Free at its Core

Every gameplay feature is free — forever. All 30+ HUD mods, performance tweaks, minimap, waypoints, and quality-of-life tools ship with the client at no cost. Optional cosmetics exist but will never gate useful features.

03

Multi-Version Support

Zeb Client supports a wide range of Minecraft versions. One launcher, one install, every version ready. When Mojang ships an update, Zeb follows — usually within days.

04

Indie & Proud

Zeb Client is an indie project built with passion, not profit. That means faster iterations, honest communication, and a real relationship with the community. You talk, we listen, we build.
